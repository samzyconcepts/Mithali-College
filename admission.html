Admission

ADMISSION PROCESS

Applications are invited from suitably qualified candidates for admission into 2020/2021 academic session application on forms are obtainable online. The admission form is five thousand naira (#5000) only. This amount also includes the cost of downloading past question from the website.

REQUIREMENTS FOR THE CANDIDATES

Those who will be eligible for admission into jss1 for 2020/2021 academic session must be Ten (10) years old by September, 2020 candidates will be required to present originals and photocopies of their birth certificate during interview.

HOW TO APPLY FOR MITHALI COLLEGE FROM

1. Fill the admission payment form and click on “Submit”

2. After submitting the payment form you will be presented with the payment option available the first is the online payment option, pay with your naira master card, verse card or GT payment the second is payment at any GT bank branch in Nigeria print the payment slip and pay at any GT bank

3. After payment, note down your PINCODE (without the PINCODE you will not be able to fill the admission form)

4. Visit this website to fill the admission forms

5. Go to admission menu and click on application form and verify your PIN
