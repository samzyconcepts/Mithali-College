Admission

ADMISSION PROCESS

Applications are invited from suitably qualified candidates for admission into 2022/2023 academic session application forms are available online and offline. The application form cost five thousand naira (#5000) only. This amount also includes the cost of downloading past question from the website.

ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS FOR THE CANDIDATES

Prospective students for admission into JSS1 for 2022/2023 academic session must be Ten (10) years old by September, 2022. Candidates will be required to present originals and photocopies of their birth certificate during the interview.

HOW TO APPLY FOR MITHALI COLLEGE FORM

Our aim is to produce the best, this means that the admission policy has been carefully designed to lay the foundation for excellence. All prospective students will write an entrance examination, they will also be required to attend an interview, to ensure the best is given a chance. A cut off mark of 50 out of 100 will be the pass mark.


Admission forms can be completed and submitted online. Please ensure a digital passport sized photograph is available for upload. Original and photocopies of all documents are to be brought to the examination venue for viewing and submission.


Admission form fee of #5000 is to be deposited into the school's account and details of payment provided on the application form.
